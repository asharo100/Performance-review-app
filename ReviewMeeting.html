Core Performance Goals
Operational Excellence & Timeliness:
Objective: Planning, prioritizing & executing of all tasks assigned
Measurable Behavior: Meeting/exceeding deadlines, minimal supervision
Accountability: Proactive identification of risks/delays
Accuracy & Quality Control:
Objective: 100% Accuracy & aligned with procedures laid down
Measurable Behavior: Zero critical errors, Adheres with defined processes, conducts self-review and validation before submission of work
Accountability: Takes full ownership of quality of work and its outcomes, proactively identifies and corrects errors, and accepts feedback constructively
Client Service & Value:
Objective: Deliver exceptional client service
Measurable Behavior: Respond to client queries, escalations and requests immediately, communicates clearly and proactively on tasks with clients and vendors, anticipates client needs and identifies opportunities to add value
Accountability: Takes ownership of client deliverables, escalates risk and accepts responsibility for client outcomes

Leadership Goals
Collaboration & Culture (One Team):
Objective: Foster a collaborative, respectful and high trust work environment
Measurable Behavior: Shares information, knowledge, and best practices, supports team members during critical periods
Accountability: Takes responsibility for team outcomes, addresses conflicts, and escalates when appropriate
Mentorship & Training:
Objective: Support the development of fellow team members by sharing knowledge
Measurable Behavior: Provides guidance, training, mentorship, shares role specific knowledge and best practices, demonstrates patience and clarity when explaining tasks or concepts
Accountability: Takes ownership of the growth and learning of those supported
Skill Development & Continuous Improvement:
Objective: Continuously enhance professional skills and knowledge
Measurable Behavior: Pursue new skill development relevant to the role
Accountability: Takes responsibility for personal development and learning
