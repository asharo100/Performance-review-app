TrueNorth Performance Review

Employee Self-Evaluation

Period: 01/01/2026 - 06/30/2026

This review is intended to support professional growth, alignment with TrueNorth values, and fair performance assessment.

Instructions for Employee Reflection:

  • Provide specific examples using the SBO (Situation-Behavior-Outcome) format
  • Define your own goals in SMART format (Specific-Measurable-Achievable-Relevant-Time Bound)
  • Be honest and constructive. Use the self-evaluation form as your opportunity to highlight your impact and discuss your growth
  • Anchor each of your self-evaluation, achievement, or example to one of the 12 boxes formed by intersection of our Values and Pillars
