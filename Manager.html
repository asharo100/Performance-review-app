Manager Review Guidelines
This review is intended to ensure fair, consistent, and objective evaluation of employee performance, while reinforcing TrueNorth values and expectations.
Instructions for Manager Evaluation:
  • Evaluate performance based on observable behaviors and measurable outcomes, not assumptions
  • Be objective and balanced — recognize strengths while clearly identifying areas of improvement
  • Provide actionable feedback that supports employee development and future growth
  • Maintain consistency and fairness across all evaluations
